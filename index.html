About Alden

After years of dreaming and fabricating of ideas, her passion and drive to serve families finally came to fruition for Chef Alden. Her vision was to create a service that fit into a busy families schedule, that allowed families to come together over a home cooked meal without the stress of it all.

Alden grew up in a large family in Springhill, SC, where food was more than just a meal - cooking was an art! Her grandfather, who spent much of his time in Paris, had exquisite taste in food which Alden quickly picked up. After traveling to France as a teenager, her curiosity about food grew as she witnessed first-hand the dynamic tastes that could be created with the simplest ingredients.

Alden first began her career as a chef in Charlotte, NC, attending Johnson and Whales University where she graduated top of her class in the Culinary Arts School. Her first job was working as an intern with M5, a Mediterranean Restaurant, while also working at Pasta and Provisions, a gourmet pasta shop in Charlotte, NC, where she expanded on her knowledge of Italian cuisine. Alden did nothing but impress with her sharp ideas, expressive creativity, contagious excitement and outright passion for food and hospitality.

She later worked as a sous chef for Travinia Italian Kitchen in Columbia, SC, where her fresh, inventive way of thinking about food quickly had her cooking for the executive owners and eventually changing and improving menu items. Alden traveled throughout SC and NC, training and inspiring young chefs for the company, revitalizing and standardizing the recipes for the Travinia menu.
